The Selected_CNF.zip contains all feasible (i.e., solved under 15 min with default EduSAT heuristics) problems from SAT 2002 / 2003 / 2009 competitions. 
In the directory feasible_by_runtime they are sorted by runtime (symbolic links to the 'feasible' directory).

Altogether it 1.3 GB and contains 563 files (141 of them take longer than 1 second to solve).

It is public on github: https://github.com/gabay/SAT-CNF-repository

Courtesy of Roi Gabay (mail from 27.1.20).
